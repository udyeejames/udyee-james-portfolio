Who I Am

About Me

I enjoy working with data and figuring out what is really going on beneath the surface. There is something satisfying about starting with a chaotic spreadsheet and walking away with a clear picture of what is happening and why.

I started with Microsoft Excel, learning how to clean datasets, spot inconsistencies, and build formulas that made sense. Over time I moved into Power BI, where I now build dashboards that help teams see their numbers clearly and act on them confidently.

What I have noticed across every project is that the most important step happens before any tool is even opened. Asking the right questions, understanding the context, and knowing what outcome would actually be useful. That is where the real work begins.

I pay close attention to detail and I take pride in delivering analysis that is not just accurate but also easy to understand and act on. Whether it is tracking sales performance or understanding why customers behave a certain way, I am focused on turning raw data into decisions.

Sales Analysis

Sales Decline and Performance Analysis of Elite Crust Bakery

The bakery was seeing a drop in revenue and needed to understand what was driving it. I went through the sales data, cleaned it up, and built a dashboard in Power BI that made it easy to see exactly where things were going wrong.

  • Cleaned and structured the raw sales dataset in Excel
  • Tracked total revenue, product performance, and sales trends over time
  • Identified which products were underperforming and why
  • Highlighted changes in customer demand affecting overall revenue
  • Delivered a dashboard that made the problem visible and actionable

Revenue and Booking Analysis

Hotel Revenue Analysis: Tracking Revenue Performance, Occupancy, Pricing and Booking Behavior

High cancellation rates were eating into the hotel's revenue and no one had a clear picture of why. I analysed booking patterns, customer behavior, and timing to build a dashboard that told the full story.

  • Analysed cancellation rates, lead time, and customer segments
  • Built a Power BI dashboard tracking occupancy, pricing, and booking behavior
  • Identified which customer types were most likely to cancel and when
  • Showed how booking timing directly impacted revenue
  • Provided insights that can help improve booking strategies and reduce losses
